# Modelling a Ballbot robot: Part 1

Last year I got to work on a ballbot robot for my capstone course at college. Now, when I first got told about this project, my mind immediately went to the oh-so familiar BB-8 from *Star Wars: The Force Awakens* (I seriously can't believe that movie came out in 2015).

  ![File:BB-8, Star Wars The Force Awakens.jpg](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/en/3/39/BB-8%2C_Star_Wars_The_Force_Awakens.jpg)

I also thought back to the massive amount of build tutorials published for making your own BB-8 robot and thought (naively!) that this would be quite an easy project. However, once I got the project specs, it turned out we actually had to make something more like this:

![img](https://miro.medium.com/max/1986/1*VYEtu9EDlg1PVujcFL7nlw.jpeg)

The robot pictured is Rezero, a seriously amazing piece of engineering work created by a group of 13 students for a bachelor thesis in ETH Zurich.
